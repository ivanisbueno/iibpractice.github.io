Welcome to the 90's!

a time to remember

Remember the 90's when websites looked super horribly designed? Even websites like Google sucked! It seems like people didn't even have sound principles of design back then. It was a big mashup of everything goes.

Now, let's talk about those busy backgrounds and hard to read text because there were no overlays used. I really don't know what people in the 90's were thinking and I am sure glad we've come a long way!
